Our Story

A Sanctuary Above the World

Nestled at 2,400 metres in the heart of the BluePeak Range, our resort was born from a singular vision: to offer guests an unrivalled communion with nature without sacrificing any comfort. Since opening in 2001, we have welcomed discerning travellers from every corner of the globe.

Our architecture honours the mountain landscape — raw stone, aged timber, panoramic glass — while interiors speak a language of quiet opulence. Every detail, from the hand-selected linens to the curated cellar, is chosen with intention.

"To create moments of stillness and wonder that guests carry with them long after they descend the mountain."
